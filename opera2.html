LUD

(recit.)

Poor child, where will she go? What will she do?

JUL

That isn't in your part, you know.

LUD

(sighing)

Quite true! Depressing topics we'll not touch upon— Let us begin as we are going on!

JUL

But stay—your new-made Court Without a courtly coat is— We shall require Some Court attire, And at a moment's notice. In clothes of common sort Your courtiers must not grovel— Your new noblesse Must have a dress Original and novel!
